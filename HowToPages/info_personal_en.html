  • Alert

    Hold the Alerty button or the "play/pause"-button on your headset for 3 seconds to send an alert

  • What will happen?

    When you send an alert, a message is sent to one or more contacts you've chosen earlier.

    The message contains an URL that provides your contacts with a map, that's updated every 10 seconds and shows your current location. The camera is recording during the time the alert is active. The alert is also visible for the administrator on the website your business account is connected to. Please note that Alerty is a silent alarm, no audible signal will sound when activated.

  • Cancel

    Slide the button and enter your authorization code to cancel an alert.

    Please note that Alerty is a silent alarm; no audible signal will sound when activated.

Settings
  • Friends

    To add a friend from your contact list, press "Add friend". It costs 1 credit to add 1 friend. Be sure to only add numbers that can receive SMS.

    To remove a friend, press the red button to the left of the friends name. A confirmation-box will then appear which will allow you to confirm the removal of this friend.

    The friends you have in the list will receive the any alerts you might send.

  • Facebook

    You can connect Alerty to Facebook by pressing the button "Connect". Eventual alerts you send will then appear in your news feed on Facebook. Your friends will see a message that you've sent an alert, as well as receive an URL to a map that shows your current location.

  • Headset

    You can activate Alerty by using a bluetooth handsfree. In this menu you can chose to activate this features, as well as conduct a test to see whether your handsfree works or not.

    By pressing "Test headset" you're starting a test where you can make sure that your handsfree works and is compatible with Alerty. Please note that not all bluetooth handsfrees are supported by Alerty, it's highly recommended that you conduct a test the first time you connect a new handsfree.

    To be able to activate an alert using your headset Alerty must be running. Because of this the indicator for location services is always visible when the application is running, even when it's sent to background.

  • Indoor locationing

    When you're inside a building it can be difficult for the iPhones GPS to acquire an exact position. To improve your positions accuracy in these situations you can connect to a wireless network. You can then set the location of this wireless network and where you are on a map.

    When you add a new wireless network you can add a description for this position. The description should describe where the network is located with as few words as possible.

    For example: "Apartment number 25" or "Office floor 2".

  • User settings

    In "user settings" you can change the name that's displayed to your friends when you send an alert. You can also change the authorization code used to cancel an alert. Finally, you can see a preview of the alert which will be sent to your friends, and possibly on Facebook, when activated.

  • Previous alerts

    All your previously sent alerts are listed in "Previous alerts". Each alert shows the date, time and from which address the alert was sent from. If you open an alert you can see when it was accepted, who accepted it and when it was canceled. You can also see a map which shows the route you took during the time the alert was active. The video that was recorded during the time the alert was active is also available here.

    In Alerty only a compressed version of the video is displayed. To access a more high resolution video, please contact us at alerty@innovationpush.com

  • Buy credits

    To add a friend you need a credit. When you're out of credits you can buy more in this view. Credits are sold in two versions, 1 credit for $0.99 or 5 credits for $2.99.
