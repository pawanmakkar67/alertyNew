  • Alert

    Hold the Alerty button or play/pause on your headset for 3 seconds to send an alert.

  • What will happen?

    When you send an alert, a message is sent to one or more contacts you’ve chosen earlier.

    The message contains an URL that provides your contacts with a map, that’s updated every 10 seconds and shows your current location. The camera is recording during the time the alert is active. The alert is also visible for the administrator on the website your business account is connected to. Please note that Alerty is a silent alarm, no audible signal will sound when activated.

  • Cancel

    Slide the button and enter your authorization code to cancel an alert.

    • Settings
  • Contacts

    To add a contact from your contact list, press “Add contact”. Be sure to only add numbers that can receive SMS.

    To remove a contact, press the red button to the left of the contacts name. A confirmation-box will then appear, which will allow you to confirm that you want to remove this contact.

    The contacts you have in the list will receive the eventual alerts you might send.

  • Headset

    You can activate Alerty by using a bluetooth handsfree. In this menu you can chose to activate this features, as well as conduct a test to see whether your handsfree works or not.

    By pressing “Test headset” you’re starting a test where you can make sure that your handsfree works and are compatible with Alerty. Please note that not all bluetooth handsfrees are supported by Alerty, it’s highly recommended that you conduct a test the first time you connect a new handsfree.

    To be able to activate an alert using your headset Alerty must be running. Because of this the indicator for location services is always visible when the application is running, even when it’s sent to background.

  • Indoor locationing

    When you’re inside a building it can be difficult for the iPhones GPS to acquire an exact position. To improve your positions accuracy in these situations you can connect to a wireless network. You can then set the location of this wireless network and where you are on a map.

    When you add a new wireless network you can add a description for this position. The description should describe where the network is located with as few words as possible.

    For example: "Apartment number 25" or "Office floor 2".

  • User settings

    In user settings you can change the account-ID that connects you to a user. You can also change the authorization code that’s used to cancel an alert. There is also a preview of the message that will be sent to your contacts if you activate an alert.

  • Previous alerts

    In previous alerts, all alerts you’ve sent are listed. Each alert shows at what date, time as well as at what adress the alert was sent from. If you open an alert you can also see when an alert was accepted, who accepted it and when it was canceled. You can also see a map which shows the route you’ve taken during the time the alert was active. The video that was recorded during the time the alert was active is also available here.

    In Alerty only a compressed version of the video is displayed. To access a more high resolution video, please contact us at alerty@innovationpush.com
